Fried Chicken

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Paprika
  • Salt and pepper
  • Buttermilk
  • Vegetable oil
  • Chicken
  1. Combine salt, pepper, paprika, and enough flour to coat the chicken in a zip-loc bag, then shake.
  2. Pour buttermilk into a shallow bowl and dip each piece of chicken.
  3. Place each piece of chicken into the zip-loc bag and shake until each piece is evenly coated.
  4. Lay the chicken on a baking sheet and then cover.
  5. Bring vegetable oil to high heat in a skillet.
  6. Brown chicken on both sides, then reduce heat and cook chicken for 30 minutes.
  7. After 30 minutes, turn the heat to high again and cook uncovered until crispy.
  8. Lay each piece on a plate of paper towels.
