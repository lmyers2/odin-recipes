Mac 'N Cheese

Description

My favorite side to any meal!

Ingredients

  • Elbow macaroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Salt and pepper
  • Milk
  • Half and half
  • Shredded cheddar cheese
  1. Preheat oven to 325 and grease a square baking dish.
  2. Boil macaroni until slightly undercooked, then drain and set aside.
  3. Melt butter over medium heat, and blend in the flour, salt, and pepper. Cook for 2 minutes.
  4. Add milk and half and half and stir slowly.
  5. Add shredded cheese and stir until melted.
  6. Toss cooked macaroni noodles in sauce.
  7. Pour half of the mixture into the greased baking dish, sprinkle 1/2 cup of cheese, pour other half in, and sprinkle with more cheese.
  8. Bake until desired gooeyness or for 10 minutes.
