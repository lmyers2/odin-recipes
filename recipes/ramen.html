Instant Noodles

Description

Very quick, very yummy!

Ingredients

  • Dry noodles
  • Seasoning packet
  • Meat
  • Water
  1. Boil water on high heat.
  2. Add noodles, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add seasoning while cooking.
  4. Remove from heat when noodles are soft.
  5. Add extra seasoning to taste.
